# Overtone Research

![Overtone Research event-risk collage](./assets/overtone-readme-banner.png)

Overtone Research is an AI-native research lab for markets, events, and collective intelligence.

We study how language models, market data, and structured reasoning can turn fragmented information into systems for understanding uncertainty.

Our work starts from a simple view: modern markets do not only price assets. They also price beliefs, narratives, constraints, and future states of the world.

## Research Directions

- AI-native market intelligence.
- Event-risk and uncertainty modeling.
- Collective belief and prediction-market structure.
- Semantic systems for mapping narratives, assets, and risks.
- Early-warning methods for complex, fast-moving information surfaces.

## Current Experiments

- Structuring fragmented event information into machine-readable objects.
- Building small technical experiments around market semantics and risk surfaces.
- Exploring how prediction-market data can inform broader event-risk research.
- Developing early methodology for indices, monitors, and warning systems.

## Principles

- Technical experiments before broad claims.
- Methodology over black-box scores.
- Structured uncertainty over false precision.
- Research artifacts over promotional noise.
- Research only. No investment advice, execution, custody, or return promises.
