# Overtone Research

![Overtone Research event-risk collage](./assets/overtone-readme-banner.png)

Overtone Research is a lab for AI-native event-risk research.

We build small technical experiments that turn fragmented event, market, and asset information into readable risk warnings, exposure watchlists, and early index methodology.

Our current focus is crypto event risk because the surface is fast-moving, narrative-heavy, and sensitive to policy, protocol, liquidity, security, and adoption events.

## What We Are Exploring

- Event-risk monitoring across fragmented market information.
- AI-assisted structuring of messy market language and resolution logic.
- Research-style exposure watchlists and risk warnings.
- Early methodology for broader all-asset indices and prediction / early-warning outputs.

## Current Principles

- Technical experiments before mature index claims.
- Risk warnings and structured observations before trade recommendations.
- Methodology, components, liquidity, and uncertainty over black-box scores.
- Research only. No investment advice, execution, custody, or return promises.

## Public Outputs

This organization will host polished research artifacts:

- methodology notes
- schemas and clean examples
- selected reproducible experiments
- small dashboards or reports
- public-facing documentation

Internal scratch work, failed experiments, and unfinished automation stay private until they can be distilled into a useful research artifact.
